Lemonade


Cooking time: 10minutes serving: 6

*Ingredient*

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 cups cold water, to dilute


*Direction*



  • Step1 :Place the sugar and water in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer. Stir so that the sugar dissolves completely and remove from heat.

  • Step2 :While the water is heating for the simple syrup, juice your lemons. Depending on the size of the lemons, 4 to 6 of them should be enough for 1 cup of juice.

  • Step3: Pour the juice and the simple syrup sugar water into a serving pitcher. Add 2 to 3 cups of cold water and taste. Add more water if you would like it to be more diluted
